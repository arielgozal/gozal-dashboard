# Gozal — condensed brand brief for the cloud agent

**What Gozal is:** a free two-sided app connecting homeowners with local service pros (plumbers, cleaners, handymen, HVAC). No fees for anyone. No lead credits. No middleman. Competitors (Thumbtack, Angi, TaskRabbit) charge vendors per lead; vendors hate it.

**The message in everything:** "No fees. No middleman. Tap and go."
- Vendor version: "Keep 100% of what you earn. No monthly credits. No per-lead fees. Just jobs."
- Consumer version: "Book a pro in minutes. No service fees. No hidden charges. Just fixed."

**Two audiences — every idea and script MUST be tagged one of these:**
- **vendor** — trades pros burned by lead fees. Recruit them to join.
- **consumer** — homeowners, property managers, short-term-rental hosts. Drive app downloads/usage.

**Content pillars (tag each idea with one):** Social Proof · Education · Behind the Pros · Problem/Solution

**Voice — always:** direct, funny welcome, scrappy underdog energy. Short sentences. Show the pain before the solution. Be specific ("3 days waiting for a callback" beats "finding pros is frustrating"). Have a point of view: lead fees are a ripoff, say so.

**Voice — never:**
- No em dashes. Use periods or commas.
- No AI words: seamless, leverage, elevate, unlock, empower, delve, landscape, robust, streamline, ecosystem, game-changer, revolutionize.
- No corporate speak, no puffery ("we're thrilled"), no filler openers ("In today's world").
- No hedging. No questions as hooks unless sharp and specific.
- NEVER name a city, county, or region in any copy, hashtag, or overlay. Content must feel local without being geographically pinned.

**Platform tone:** TikTok = loud, fast, hook in 2 seconds. Reels/carousels = real and useful, screenshot-worthy. Facebook/Nextdoor = neighbor-mode, helpful first, Gozal mentioned once and naturally, never as an ad.

**Real vendor quotes (use these as hooks, they are gold):**
- "I pay $1,500-2,000 a month and maybe 20% of leads convert."
- "Angi sold my lead to 5 other contractors. That's not a lead, that's a lottery."
- "I'm paying to compete against guys with fake reviews."
- "Every time I get a good lead, they raise the price."

**Real consumer quotes:**
- "I called 3 people. Two never called back. One was booked two weeks out."
- "They charge a service fee on top of what the pro charges. Found out at checkout."
- "It said top pro and then he didn't show up."
